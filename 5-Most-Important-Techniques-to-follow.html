SEO Guide for Beginner: 5 Most Important Techniques to follow

          In the years 2020 and 2021, the COVID 19 pandemic has brought lots of changes to people's lives. We now depend on the internet more than ever. Most businesses have gone online to grab more attention because people have changed their behavior over the years to do things online. As a result, people have got countless options to choose from for anything. This has increased the competition as well for online small businesses. Everyone in this competition is trying to gain more profit than others and exactly here comes the importance of SEO.

👉🏼    What is SEO?

           SEO or Search Engine Optimization is the process to make the webpage Optimized according to the Search Engine Algorithms. By doing so, the search engine will prioritize your pages and it will bring the page to the first page results, and finally to the top of the 1st page. This will increase the visibility and accessibility of your web pages.

          These techniques of SEO are always changing as Search Engines are always improving their algorithms according to people’s behavior, trends, and a lot more other parameters. Keeping all those things in mind, we too need to change our strategies of doing SEO to stand out from the crowd. Instead of these factors, some SEO strategies are evergreen. Those are always important, working, and result-driven.

         Here in this blog, I'll be discussing the 5 most important evergreen techniques of SEO for beginners.

First Step to SEO is Keyword Research

1.    Keywords Research

           The first and most important step is Finding the Right Keywords for You. People usually search in Search Engines (i.e. Google, Bing, etc) using some relevant words, which are better known as keywords or keyphrases (more than one word). We must do some research on those keywords or phrases, by looking for their search volumes, competition, and relevance to make your pages SEO friendly.


For Keyword research, lots of free keyword tools are out there, for example, Wordstream, Keyword sheeter, Arefs keyword generator, etc. We must choose some most relevant Keywords or phrases, which have higher search volume and better competition.

Proper Title and Meta Description Attracts People

2.    Title and Description

           After searching in the search engine using Key phrases, a list of results appears on a page, which is known as the Search Engine Result Page, in short SERP. From those people go through the list of titles and the description of the sites, which is better known as Meta Description. These things should also be optimized. A title should not be incomplete in the SERP. If the title of the page has less than 61 characters it will show in full else it will show some dots instead of those extra characters, which implies that the title is too long to show and that doesn’t look good. The same happens with the Meta Description as well. Although meta description can provide space for 155 characters. There are some free tools available online for this too, viz. Counting Characters, Moz Title Tag, etc. An optimized title tag should be in a format as Shown below


           Company Name: Primary Key-phrase | Secondary Key-phrase | Call to Action Phrase


           The meta description must contain the Keywords or Phrases. Because a search engine gives importance to the meta description first. Although nowadays, Google assigns some of the contents from the concerned webpage if the meta description is not present. But still, providing meta descriptions is important as it improves SEO ranking.

Interactive Webpages Make Dwell-Time Longer

3.    Make the Page More Interactive

           To gain more business, we need more conversion rates and to do so we need more customers to stay on our web pages. People don't usually like content only, black and white web pages. This will never give you what you want from your website.


           How to Improve? Think of yourself as a visitor, what would you like? Would you like to read a 2k or 3k word content, will that be enough attention-seeking for you? Of Course Not!! Who likes those black and white old-fashioned words!!

           Let's think about pictures, we must include some interactive, Colorful pictures, which will speak more than words only. We must think about some more call-to-action buttons. People always like it when a page is more like talking to them or which engages them to do something. More call to action button means more interactions.


           So, word content is not important? Of course, it is important, as a search engine does not see pictures, it sees content, keywords, and frequency of keywords. A webpage must have 300 to 500 words of content which must include 10 to 30 Keywords. Search Engines will crawl for those keywords in your web pages to prioritize your URL in SERP (Search Engine Result Page). 

Page Speed is Very Important Factor to Reduce Bounce Rate

4.    Page Speed

          It is assumed that as website owners we have only 5 seconds to convince a visitor to stay on our landing page!! So it is always better to optimize the contents of the page to load as fast as possible. Less loading time means a better chance to increase your conversion rate!!


          Also nowadays Google is giving more priority to those fast-loading sites over the slower ones. This also applies to every type of device, including iOs, Android, and other screen sizes.


         On the internet some free web page speed checker tools are available, i.e. webpagetest, pingdom, etc.

Image Description

5.    More More and More Analysis

           Reaching the top of the SERP is not a one-day job. It takes time. We need to analyze our domains regularly so that we can consistently keep our rank improving. There are lots of free tools available on the internet, which helps us with the analysis. For example, Google Search Console and Google Analytics are the most popular ones, except these GoSquared, Woopra, FoxMetrix are also available!!


           We also need to keep records of every changes made to the websites so that end of a certain period we can measure the ultimate result, where we started and where we are now, what were the key steps towards the success, what are the rooms of improvement et cetra.

👉🏼    Conclusion

             These are the only 5 of Thousands of SEO techniques, but these come under some of the most important ones. We need to update ourselves with the latest trends and strategies of SEO to accelerate the process of reaching the top of the first SERP and also to keep our knowledge up-to-date. Also, we need to keep practicing SEO techniques regularly, because there’s an English proverb that says, “Practice Makes You Perfect.”
